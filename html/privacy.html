Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to me. Accordingly, I have developed this Policy in order for you to understand how I collect, use, communicate and disclose and make use of personal information in MapTrek application. The following outlines application privacy policy.

  • Application does not collect any personal information.
  • Application collects technical information including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile operating system, the way you use application.
  • Such information is collected and used solely for development purposes.
  • Collected information is not shared to any third parties.
  • No real time location information is collected by application except for optional Location Share Plugin. In later case such information is stored for not more than 24 hours.
  • Some aggregated and anonymized location information can be collected in order to improve provided service.
  • All information is collected by lawful and fair means and, where appropriate, with the knowledge or consent of the individual concerned.
  • Collected information is protected by reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.

I am committed to conducting my development in accordance with these principles in order to ensure that the confidentiality of personal information is protected and maintained.
